What is Panicomix?

Panicomix is a cosplay festival, which contains a lot of different activities. You'll find different stands about related to comics, films, shows and anime.
There also will fair with merchandise both official and those who was made by artists. There also will be few lectures related to comics and cosplay.
But the major part of the event is cosplay contest!

When it will be?

May 25, 2024

Where?

Metro Toronto Convention Centre
255 Front St W, Toronto, ON M5V 2W6

Aisha Mann is also known as Deidralis.
Into cosplay since 2010. She is a winner of several conventions,
such as San-Diego Comic-Con, Anime NYC, Japan Expo, BlizzCon, etc.
Has more than 50k followers in instagram and managed to monetize her hobby.

In cosplay, she appreciates attention to details, similarity to characters
and well-staged performance.

There will be a lecture on festival, where Aisha will tell about how to stage
your performance, how it looks from audience. She will also give few tips
for the beginners.

Jennifer Vance is a new star in comics industry.
She already worked with few major publishers and was a co-writer for few story arcs.
Jennifer also has experience as a main writer for comics story arc.

Now she wants to present her new project called "Moon Wind".
Jennifer will be happy to see everyone on her Q&A panel and answer your question.
